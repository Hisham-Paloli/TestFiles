Services Overview

computer lab

The well equipped computer lab helps students learn the computer technology as part of their studies. It also help them to access the online classes, and online resources to support their curriculum.

Library

In the fond memory of former Chief Minister Jb. CH Muhammed Koya, the college library is named “CH Memorial Library”. The library contains good number of books, magazines, newspapers and other resources in various disciplines.

Smart room

Smart rooms facilitate the technology enhanced with audio-visual facilities, that helps in teaching and learning process. Audio-visual equipments facilitate video capturing of the online classes especially during the Covid-19 pandemic.

Auditorium

The new auditorium, facilitates to organise various programmes like workshop, seminar, and cultural events of students.

Hostel

The Hidayathul Islam Da’wa College is a fully residential campus, and all students and faculty members residing in the campus and are provided with free hostel facility inside the campus.

Sports Facilities

College provides ample scope for sports and physical exercise for the students. Students use play-ground for football, cricket, badminton and other sports items.

About Us

Hidayathul Islam Da’wa College , one of the prestigious Islamic initiatives of southern Kerala, located in shore of Arabian sea at Kovalam which affiliated to Darul Huda Islamic University.
